title: User Experience
short: Provide an accessible, understandable, and trustworthy interaction for end users.
---

Set interaction guidelines for tone of voice, clarity, source display, and uncertainty. Define how accessibility and comprehension are tested with representative users - including low digital-skill audiences.

A good assistant tells the user what it does *not* know as clearly as what it does.
